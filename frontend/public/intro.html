COMPONENT LAB

Components You Can Build With

Explore supported sensors, actuators, displays, controllers, and communication modules.

MICROCONTROLLER

Arduino Uno R3

The educational standard. 5V logic level, 14 digital I/O pins, and 6 analog inputs. Great for local sensors.

Voltage: 5V | Clock: 16MHz
MCU + WIFI

ESP32 DevKitC

The IoT powerhouse. 3.3V logic level with integrated 2.4GHz Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Direct cloud connection.

Voltage: 3.3V | Dual Core
INPUT SENSOR

PIR Motion (HC-SR501)

Measures infrared heat signatures. Emits a HIGH pulse when human/animal motion is detected within 7 meters.

Angle: 110° | VCC: 5V
INPUT SENSOR

Soil Moisture Sensor

Measures soil resistance to estimate volumetric water content. Returns analog values to determine dry levels.

Type: Analog | Output: 0-1023
OUTPUT ACTUATOR

5V Water Pump

DC submersible mini water pump. Draws substantial current, requiring a transistor switch (EIL enforces this).

Current: ~150mA | VCC: 5V
OUTPUT ACTUATOR

Active Buzzer

Piezoelectric audio transducer. Emits a continuous tone when supplied power. Requires limit series resistor.

Type: Digital | VCC: 5V
OUTPUT DISPLAY

RGB Common Cathode LED

Three colored LEDs combined. Driven by PWM signals to mix red, green, and blue. Requires 220Ω series resistors.

Type: PWM | Channels: R,G,B
INPUT SENSOR

Photoresistor (LDR)

Light-dependent resistor. Resistance decreases in high light. Configured with a voltage divider circuit.

Type: Analog | Divider R: 10kΩ
